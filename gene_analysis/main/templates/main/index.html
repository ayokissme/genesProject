About the service

FindGen is an integrated, searchable database that provides comprehensive and user-friendly information about human genes. When searching for a gene, neighbors are also given. The service builds diagrams that can be scaled, save images for use in scientific articles, and much more.

There are more than 12,000 genes in the database of our service
